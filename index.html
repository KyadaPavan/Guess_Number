Day-by-Day Adventure

Day 1: Road Trip to Lonavala

Tiger Point & Bhushi Dam

₹1,200

Start your epic journey early morning from Gujarat. Reach Lonavala by afternoon and check into your accommodation. Visit the spectacular Tiger Point for panoramic valley views, then cool off at Bhushi Dam - perfect for photos and some splashing around with friends!

Day 2: Heritage & Nature

Karla Caves, Sunset Point, Rajmachi Fort

₹1,100

Explore ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves at Karla Caves, marvel at the intricate architecture dating back to 2nd century BC. Trek to Rajmachi Fort for adventure and history, then end the day with breathtaking sunset views that'll make your Instagram pop!

Day 3: Imagica Theme Park

Nitro, Deep Space, Scream Machine & More!

₹2,200

The ultimate thrill day! Experience India's most exciting theme park with heart-pounding rides like Nitro (hyper coaster), Deep Space (indoor multi-dimensional coaster), Scream Machine, and Dare 2 Drop. Perfect for adrenaline junkies and creating unforgettable memories!

Day 4: Chill & Return

Wax Museum & Brunch

₹550

Relaxed final day with a leisurely brunch, visit the local wax museum for some fun photo ops, pick up souvenirs, and begin your journey back to Gujarat with hearts full of amazing memories!
